#### Bacteroides_thetaiotaomicron
    - Has four BGS's that encode the classes:
        - BGC 1.1: RRE-containing
        - BGC 1.2: RRE-containing
        - BGC 1.3: Phosphonate
        - BGC 1.4: Terpene-precurser

Only BGC 1.3 is identified by KownClusterBlast. The match also produces a compound with phosphor so yes
we believe so.

We found 71 annotated genomes of Bacteroides thetaiotaomicron in the antiSMASH database. The strains do share BGS's. Many of them share the terpene precuser and RRE-containing BGC's

#### Bifidobacterium_infantis
    - Has four BGS's that encode the classes:
        - BGC 1.1: RiPP-like
        - BGC 1.2: Lanthipeptide-class-iii or lassopeptide
        - BGC 1.3: Terpene-precurser
        - BGC 1.4: RiPP-like

None of the BGC's are identified by KnownClusterBlast

We could not find this specific species of Bifidobacterium in the antiSMASH database

#### Lactobacillus_crispatus
    - Has three BGS's that encode the classes:
        - BGC 1.1: RiPP-like
        - BGC 1.2: Terpene-precurser
        - BGC 1.3: RiPP-like

BGC 1.3 is identified by KnownClusterBlast. The match we get is not to the core biosynthetic genes, but to
one of the unkown "other" genes of the BGC, so the BGC probably does not produce the same compounds as the match identified by KnownClusterBlast

We found 34 genomes/strains of Lactobacillus crispatus. They also share some BGC's with out own sample.
